we protect your privacy, This declares that how we collect your information and use it for your purposes.

When you use (install and register) Thamao Android applications we collect or ask you to provide certain information in order to help us manage our relationship with you. This includes information such as,Name, Email ID, Mobile number, Equipment identifier (IMEI), (Barcode Images) , Subscriber identifier (IMSI), Device information(Device name, Device type, Manufacturer, OS version and Language).
